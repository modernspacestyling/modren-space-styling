Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information

Modern Space Styling
Pty Ltd · Staging · Photography · Geelong VIC

1. About this policy

Modern Space Styling Pty Ltd (ABN 75 685 915 495) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and disclose personal information when you visit our website, use our Modern Space Styling mobile application (iOS and Android), or engage our property staging and photography services in Geelong, Victoria, Australia. We comply with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

2. What personal information we collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with property staging, photography and design consultation services. Specifically:

  • Name — to identify you and address you correctly in communications.
  • Email address — to send booking confirmations, quotes, invoices and service updates, and to verify your identity when you sign in to view your bookings (see section 2A below).
  • Phone number — to contact you about your booking, installation timing and on-site logistics.
  • Property address — the location where staging or photography services are to be performed.
  • Real estate agency name (optional) — provided by agents booking on behalf of vendors.
  • Property access details (optional) — for example, a lockbox code provided by the agent so our installation team can access the property. Stored encrypted at rest (AES-256-GCM) and only accessible to authorised staging personnel.
  • Booking-related details — such as preferred date, number of bedrooms, package selected and any notes you provide.
  • App usage data — anonymised crash logs and basic interaction events from our mobile app, used solely to diagnose technical issues and improve reliability.

We do not collect financial account numbers, payment card details, government identifiers, biometric data, precise location data, photos, contacts, calendar entries, health information, or browsing history through our website or app.

2A. Optional sign-in (passwordless email verification)

From version 1.1 of our mobile app, customers can optionally sign in using a passwordless "magic link" sent to their email address. Signing in is not required to book — it's offered purely as a convenience so that previous bookings appear in one place and form fields can be pre-filled on subsequent enquiries.

If you choose to sign in:

  • We use your email address as the unique identifier for your account. No password is collected or stored.
  • We send a single-use sign-in link to your inbox, valid for one hour. Clicking the link signs you in on the device that requested it.
  • An authenticated session token is stored locally on your device. It is never shared with third parties or used for tracking outside the Modern Space Styling app.
  • We do not collect or store passwords because the magic-link flow does not use them.

You can sign out at any time from the "My Bookings" tab. Signing out clears the session token from your device. To delete the account itself, email us per section 7 below.

3. Why we collect this information

We use your personal information solely to:

  • Quote, schedule and deliver the physical staging, photography or consultation services you have requested.
  • Communicate with you regarding your booking and any follow-up.
  • Issue invoices and maintain financial records as required by Australian tax law.
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues in our mobile app (crash and performance data only, never linked to identity).

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, retargeting, behavioural analytics across other websites, or any other purpose beyond delivering the services you requested.

4. How we store and protect your information

Your booking and contact information is stored in our secure backend database, hosted with reputable third-party infrastructure providers (Supabase, hosted on Amazon Web Services in Sydney, Australia). All data in transit between your device, our website and our backend is encrypted using HTTPS (TLS). Access to stored data is restricted to authorised Modern Space Styling personnel using individual authenticated accounts.

5. Disclosure to third parties

We do not sell, rent, trade or disclose your personal information to third parties for marketing or any other purpose, except where strictly necessary to deliver the service you have engaged us for, or where required by Australian law:

  • Service delivery partners — for example, our photographer or removalist may receive the property address to attend your booking. They are bound by confidentiality.
  • Infrastructure providers — Supabase (database), Vercel (web hosting) and Twilio (SMS notifications) process information on our behalf under their own privacy and security commitments.
  • Legal obligations — we may disclose information if compelled by valid Australian court order, subpoena, or law enforcement request.

6. Data retention

We retain booking and contact information for as long as you are an active client and for a further seven (7) years after the most recent booking, in line with Australian Taxation Office record-keeping obligations. Anonymised app crash and performance data is retained for up to ninety (90) days for diagnostic purposes, then deleted.

7. Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any information that is inaccurate, incomplete or out of date.
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to our legal record-keeping obligations under tax law).
  • Withdraw consent to receive non-essential communications from us at any time.
  • Make a complaint about how we have handled your personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at modernspacestyling@gmail.com with the subject line "Privacy request". We will respond within thirty (30) days.

8. Children's privacy

Our services and mobile app are intended for adults aged 18 and over (real estate agents, vendors and homeowners). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. Cookies and analytics

Our website uses essential cookies required for the site to function. We do not use third-party advertising cookies, behavioural tracking pixels, or cross-site tracking. Our mobile app does not contain advertising or third-party trackers.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. The current version is always available at this URL. Material changes will be communicated by email to active clients where reasonably practical.
