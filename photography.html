Common Questions

Photography FAQ

How long does a photography session take? +
A standard session takes 30-60 minutes depending on property size. Video walkthroughs and drone sessions may take up to 90 minutes for larger properties.
When will I receive my images? +
All images are professionally edited and delivered within 24 hours via a secure download link. Rush delivery (same day) is available on request.
Do you shoot for rental properties too? +
Absolutely. Our packages work for both sale and rental listings. Quality photos attract better tenants faster and reduce vacancy periods.
Can I bundle photography with staging? +
Yes! Bundle any photography package with a staging booking and receive 10% off the photography price. Just mention it when booking or select the bundle option.
Are drone flights legal in my area? +
Our drone operators are CASA-certified and check all local regulations before every flight. Some areas (near airports, restricted zones) may limit drone use — we'll advise you at booking.
What if the weather is bad on shoot day? +
We'll reschedule at no extra cost if weather conditions (heavy rain, strong winds) prevent a quality shoot. We'll contact you the morning of the shoot if rescheduling is needed.
